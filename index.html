Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard

Welcome to the Belly Button dashboard! With this tool, participants from an anonymous study will be able to select their ID no. in the dropdown below to view the types of bacteria that colonize their belly. From the various charts below, participants will be able to view their demographic info, the top 10 bacterias that inhabit their bellybutton, the amount of bacteria cultures per sample, and the reported number of times their bellybutton is scrubbed each week.
